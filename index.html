Focused on What Matters Most

Concurrent Offloads

Maximize on-set efficiency by offloading multiple camera cards at the same time. Slate handles each job in parallel, getting you wrapped faster.

Uncompromising Verification

Trust nothing. Verify everything. Ensure perfect bit-for-bit copies with xxHash or MD5, or validate existing drives against MHL manifests. Data integrity is paramount.

Intuitive by Design

A clean, uncluttered interface means no steep learning curve. Set up complex jobs with simple drag-and-drop actions and clear controls. It just works.

Broad Camera & File Support

Slate handles media from any camera—ARRI, RED, Sony, Canon, and more. It fully supports all standard file systems like HFS+, APFS, and ExFAT without issue.

Blazing-Fast & Native

Slate is a lightweight, native macOS app. No sluggish frameworks or wasted resources—just pure performance that gets the job done and stays out of your way.

Clear, Professional Reports

Generate clean, customizable PDF reports for delivery. Add your logo, metadata, and filmstrip thumbnails for a polished handover that production will love.
